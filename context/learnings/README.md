# /context/learnings

Research memos, post-mortems, and shared insights that didn't belong in a story or a decision but are worth not losing.

## What belongs here

- Research on a topic before a decision gets made (literature reviews, framework comparisons)
- Post-mortems on incidents or failed experiments
- Generalized patterns extracted from multiple stories
- Explorations that informed current structure but aren't decisions themselves

## What does not belong here

- Active story work (→ `stories/NNN-slug/research.md`)
- Decisions we're committed to (→ `decisions/`)
- Unknowns (→ `open-questions.md`)

## Naming

```
YYYY-MM-DD-short-slug.md
```

Date-prefixed so time-order is visible. Learnings can be superseded by new learnings but the old one stays for the record.

## Format

Free-form. A learning should be re-readable by a future you who has forgotten the original context.
